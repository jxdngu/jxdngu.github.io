图像旋转

  1. 绕原点旋转

    如图所示, 点P坐标为(x, y), OP与x轴正方向的夹角为\(\alpha\), OP的长度为\(l\). 点P绕原点\(O\)顺时针旋转\(\theta\)到\(P^{'}\)为\((x^{'}, y^{'})\)

    点P的坐标表示为: \[ \begin{cases} x = l \cdot{\cos{\alpha }} \\ y = l \cdot{\sin{\alpha }} \end{cases} \]

    点\(P^{'}\)的坐标表示为: \[ \begin{cases} x^{'} & = l \cdot{\cos{(\alpha - \theta)}} \\ & = l \cdot{\cos{\alpha}\cos{\theta}} + l \cdot{\sin{\alpha}\sin{\theta}} \\ &= x cos{\theta} + y \sin{\theta} \\ y^{'} &= l \cdot{\sin{(\alpha - \theta)}} \\ &= l \cdot{\sin{\alpha}\cos{\theta}} - l \cdot{\cos{\alpha}\sin{\theta}} \\ &= - x \sin{\theta} + y cos{\theta} \end{cases} \]

    即: \[ \begin{bmatrix} x^{'} \\ y^{'} \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} cos{\theta} & sin{\theta} & 0 \\ -sin{\theta} & cos{\theta} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \cdot \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \]

  2. 绕任意点旋转

    如图所示, 点C坐标为\((x_C, y_C)\), 点P坐标为(x, y), CP与x轴正方向的夹角为\(\alpha\), CP的长度为\(l\). 点P绕点\(C\)顺时针旋转\(\theta\)到\(P^{'}\)为\((x^{'}, y^{'})\)

    点P的坐标表示为: \[ \begin{cases} x = x_C + l \cdot{\cos{\alpha }} \\ y = y_C + l \cdot{\sin{\alpha }} \end{cases} \]

    点\(P^{'}\)的坐标表示为: \[ \begin{cases} x^{'} & = x_C + l \cdot{\cos{(\alpha - \theta)}} \\ & = x_C + l \cdot{\cos{\alpha}\cos{\theta}} + l \cdot{\sin{\alpha}\sin{\theta}} \\ & = (x_C + l \cdot{\cos{\alpha }}) \cdot \cos{\theta} + (y_C + l \cdot{\sin{\alpha }}) \cdot \sin {\theta} + (x_C-x_C \cdot \cos{\theta} - y_C \cdot \sin{\theta}) \\ &= x cos{\theta} + y \sin{\theta} + (x_C-x_C \cdot \cos{\theta} - y_C \cdot \sin{\theta}) \\ y^{'} &= y_C + l \cdot{\sin{(\alpha - \theta)}} \\ &= y_C + l \cdot{\sin{\alpha}\cos{\theta}} - l \cdot{\cos{\alpha}\sin{\theta}} \\ & = (y_C + l \cdot{\sin{\alpha }}) \cdot \cos{\theta} - (x_C + l \cdot{\cos{\alpha }}) \cdot \sin {\theta} + (y_C-y_C \cdot \cos{\theta} + x_C \cdot \sin{\theta}) \\ &= - x \sin{\theta} + y cos{\theta} + (y_C-y_C \cdot \cos{\theta} + x_C \cdot \sin{\theta}) \end{cases} \]

    即: \[ \begin{bmatrix} x^{'} \\ y^{'} \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} cos{\theta} & sin{\theta} & x_C-x_C \cdot \cos{\theta} - y_C \cdot \sin{\theta} \\ -sin{\theta} & cos{\theta} & y_C-y_C \cdot \cos{\theta} + x_C \cdot \sin{\theta} \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \cdot \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \]

cv.getRotationMatrix2D(center, angle, scale)
